Who Is Teacher Ben?

Hello! I am Teacher Ben.


I am a passionate mathematics educator dedicated to the Grade 8A 25/26 Class. My philosophy is simple: Math is not just about numbers on a board; it's about understanding the patterns of the world.


Whether we are proving the Pythagorean theorem (a² + b² = c²) or analyzing geometric data, I believe in a classroom that blends traditional problem-solving with modern digital tools. My goal is to inspire "Awesome Math" moments every single day.
