About Me

Hello! I am Teacher Ben, a passionate Computer Science instructor dedicated to demystifying technology. With a career spanning over a decade, I have had the privilege of teaching thousands of students, from absolute beginners to advanced developers.


My teaching philosophy is rooted in the belief that anyone can learn to code. I specialize in making complex concepts—like algorithms, data structures, and web development—accessible and engaging. Whether we are building a simple website or solving complex logic puzzles, my classroom is a space for innovation.
